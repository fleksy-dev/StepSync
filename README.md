# StepSync
StepSync listens to the music around you and instantly recognizes which ballroom and social dance fits — from Slow Waltz and Viennese Waltz to Cha-Cha, Tango, Salsa, Jive, Quickstep and more. It shows the tempo (BPM and bars-per-minute). All audio analysis runs 100% on your device

- 🎧 Real-time dance recognition from the microphone — no typing, no guessing
- 🥁 Tempo in BPM and measures-per-minute (the way dancers count)
- 👣 Beat-synced metronome with per-dance step guidance
- 📖 Built-in dance lexicon (tempo ranges, difficulty, counting)
- 🔒 100% on-device — your audio never leaves your phone
- 🆓 Open source (Apache-2.0)
